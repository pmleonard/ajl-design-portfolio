Cheesy Chums

Services

Package Design, Illustration

Overview

Cheesy Chums is a playful, ocean-inspired snack packaging series designed for a line of cheddar crackers shaped like penguins, whales, and sea turtles. Each of the three box designs highlights a different marine animal and donates a portion of the proceeds to a conservation organization dedicated to protecting that species. The project combines bold, character-driven illustration with an educational and eco-conscious message aimed at children and families. The visual style is vibrant, created entirely in Procreate using textured brushes to give the illustrations a warm, hand-crafted feel.

Visual Design

The Cheesy Chums packaging features bold, character-driven illustrations of marine animals, each with a unique personality. The use of vibrant colors and textured digital brushes gives the boxes a warm, hand-crafted feel. The design not only appeals to children but also communicates an eco-conscious message, with each box supporting a different marine conservation effort. The playful style and educational focus make Cheesy Chums stand out on the shelf and inspire families to learn more about ocean conservation.
