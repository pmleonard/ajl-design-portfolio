Celestial Dream Map and Journal

Services

Information Design, Editorial Design, Illustration, System Design

Overview

This project explores the visualization of complex datasets by transforming dream interpretation into a comprehensive system of artifacts. The system includes a celestial-themed poster, a dream journal, and a reference bookmark. The poster maps common dream themes and their interpretations, while the journal offers space for daily entries and weekly “dreamscape” spreads where dreams can be plotted as constellations. The bookmark provides a quick guide to recurring dream categories, tying the system together as both an informative and imaginative experience.
